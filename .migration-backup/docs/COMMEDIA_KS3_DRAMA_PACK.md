# Commedia dell'arte – KS3 Drama pack (🎭)

Purchasable pack with 8 ready-to-use lessons for Year 8 Drama (viewable for any Year * Drama).

## Setup

1. **Run the migration** (once) in Supabase SQL Editor:
   - `supabase_migrations/insert_commedia_ks3_drama_pack.sql`
   - This adds the pack so it can be purchased or assigned by an admin.

2. **Year groups**: Year 3–8 Drama are in the default year groups. Select **Year 8 Drama** (or another Drama year) from the class dropdown.

3. **Categories**: In **Settings → Categories**, assign **Drama Games** to **Year 8 Drama** (and any other Year * Drama you use) so activities and lessons show correctly.

4. **Get the pack**: Purchase the pack in-app or have an admin assign it to you (Settings → Users → Edit user → Assign purchased resources).

5. **Load the lessons**: With **Year 8 Drama** selected, open **Lesson Library** and click **Load Commedia pack**. This adds 8 example lessons (Lesson 1–7 + Assessment) to your calendar. You can edit them like any other lesson.

## Lesson content

- **Lesson 1**: Introduction to CDA, audience and improvisation (Chaplin factory scene link).
- **Lesson 2**: Body language and comedy role (tension states, centres of leading, Master/Servant, animal qualities).
- **Lesson 3**: Stock characters and Lazzi routine (Il Dottore, Isabella, Harlequin, Zanni).
- **Lesson 4**: Exaggeration through masks (mask rules, burglar scene).
- **Lesson 5**: Gesture and movement (Pantalone, Harlequin, El Capitano, Zanni).
- **Lesson 6**: Creating a mask for performance.
- **Lesson 7**: Voice, masks and Columbina scene (leading to assessment).
- **Lesson 8**: Assessment (rehearsal and criteria; Part 1 + Part 2).

Example resource links (e.g. YouTube) are included in the lesson text for reference.
